LE

Ahn Hyo-jin (Hangul: 안효진; born December 10, 1991), professionally known as LE, is a South Korean rapper, producer, and songwriter. She has been a member of the K-pop girl group EXID since 2012. She was previously a member of the underground hip hop group, Jiggy Fellaz, performing under the stage name, Elly.
